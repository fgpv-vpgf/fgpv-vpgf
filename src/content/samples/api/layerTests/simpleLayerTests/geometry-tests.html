Federal Geospatial Platform Visualiser - Geometry Tests

Point Geometries Tests:

Hover over A and expect an annotation to open to the right of the point as long as the point is still being hovered on. Hover over E and expect an annotation to remain open to the left of the point even if the point is not being hovered on any longer

Hover over either point and expect no annotation to open

MultiPoint Geometries Tests:

Hover over any of the points and expect an annotation to open which will follow the cursor as long as the point is being hovered on.
